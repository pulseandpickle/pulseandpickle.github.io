Book a Table

Prior booking is not required for groups smaller than 8.
For groups of 8 or more, we offer a set menu at a fixed price per person.

When making a booking at least 48 hours in advance,
please send an email to bookings@pulseandpickle.org.


If you are looking to book a table at our restaurant
in less than 48 hours, please give us a call at 07591 476918.


The Pulse & Pickle restaurant is open 11am to 5pm.
Please see our Events page for details on extended opening hours when we host events.

Notes on our menu

Our tea bags come from Dragonfly Tea and Hampstead Tea.

Our coffee supplier is Techo Verde.

Our oat, coconut, and soy milk come from Glebe Farm.

We stock beer from Toast Brewing, Queer Brewing, and Black Isle Brewing Co.
Our beer stock includes both low alcohol and gluten-free options!
