Visit Us

34 High Street
London, E17 7LD


We're located on the Walthamstow High Street and Market Road, right around the corner from St James Place Overground Station and a 10 min walk from Walthamstow Central Station. If you prefer to cycle, there are bike racks on the side street next to us for locking bikes, and we also have a bike pump for visitors to borrow.

Our space has a step-free toilet and a street-facing, step-free entrance.

We are a dog-friendly space. Bring us your fur babies and we'll give them free water and treats!

Got any other questions, concerns, feedback, or ideas? Email us at hello@pulseandpickle.org.
